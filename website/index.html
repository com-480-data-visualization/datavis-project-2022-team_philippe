Navigating the Bubble Graph Using the informations that you gave to Philippe, he is now able to show you books that may arise your interest! To prevent recommending only mainstream content, he created this bubble graph for you to navigate:
• Genres-layer: each top-layer bubble represents a genre. The larger the bubble, the more books are inside.
• Book-layer: Clicking on a genre bubble will zoom you into it and allow you to get a bigger pictures of the books present. This time, the bigger the bubble, the more likely it is similar to books you matched in on your Tinder page!
• Window-layer: A title seems appealing? Just click the particular book bubble to display all detailed information about it! Cover, summary, price... nothing can escape you!
